Jedi Master Luke, Jedi Knight Revan, Hermit Yoda, Grand Master Yoda
If you are not actively using Jedi Knight Cal Kestis, he is better than GMY. Try to span stuns as much as possible. When you kill an enemy, another enemy will get 100% turn meter. Try to time the stuns so that the only enemy unstunned is the one you just killed. This will prevent them from making attacks which HURT. Using JKL's granted ability at the end of wave 1 will allow him to go first in wave 2 and start the stuns.
